Our Services

About Us

Welcome to Tour of Uttarakhand! We specialize in crafting unforgettable experiences amidst the breathtaking beauty of Uttarakhand.

From hotel bookings to thrilling safaris, we offer a diverse range of services tailored to make your journey truly memorable. Let us be your guide to adventure and exploration in this enchanting destination.

Escape the ordinary with our meticulously crafted tour packages. From thrilling adventures to cultural immersions, our diverse itineraries cater to every traveler's desires.

Explore hidden gems, indulge in local flavors, and create lasting memories with Tour of Uttarakhand as your trusted guide.

Services

Hotel & Resort Booking

Are you looking for a cozy mountain retreat or a luxurious resort nestled in the heart of nature? We'll find you the perfect accommodation tailored to your preferences and budget.

Conference Group

Planning a corporate retreat or team-building event amidst the serene backdrop of Uttarakhand? Let us handle all the logistics to ensure a seamless and productive experience for your group.

Wedding Planner

Dreaming of saying "I do" surrounded by the majestic Himalayas? Our wedding planning services will bring your vision to life, from selecting the ideal venue to coordinating every last detail of your special day.

Pre Wedding Shoot

Capture the magic of your love story against the stunning landscapes of Uttarakhand with our pre-wedding shoot packages. We'll help you find the most picturesque locations for unforgettable photos.

Tour Packages

Whether you're craving adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, our customized tour packages have something for everyone. Explore ancient temples, embark on thrilling jungle safaris, or simply unwind amidst nature's splendor.

Taxi Hire

Sit back, relax, and enjoy the scenic drive as our reliable taxi services take you to your desired destinations safely and comfortably.

Jungle Safari & Mountain Drive

Get ready for an exhilarating journey through the wilderness of Uttarakhand! Experience the thrill of spotting exotic wildlife and soaking in panoramic mountain views along the way.

River Rafting

Feel the adrenaline rush as you conquer the rapids of Uttarakhand's pristine rivers! Our river rafting excursions are perfect for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Canter Safari

Hop aboard our canter safari for an unforgettable wildlife adventure in Uttarakhand's national parks. Keep your camera ready to capture sightings of majestic creatures in their natural habitat.

Local Sightseeing

Immerse yourself in the rich culture and heritage of Uttarakhand with our guided local sightseeing tours. Explore charming villages, ancient temples, and hidden gems off the beaten path.

Elephant Safari & Camel Ride

Embark on a unique safari experience atop gentle giants or graceful camels. Traverse the rugged terrain of Uttarakhand with these majestic creatures as your companions.
